Cancer

Subversive and streetwear-infused, blending elements of traditional menswear with a contemporary urban edge, characterized by bold graphics, unexpected proportions, and a nod to British subcultures.

Cancers are known for their nurturing nature, emotional depth, and attachment to home and tradition. Martine Rose's brand often reflects a sense of intimacy and community, much like Cancer's emphasis on family and close relationships. The brand's designs can evoke a sense of nostalgia and familiarity, drawing inspiration from cultural references and subcultures. Martine Rose's aesthetic embraces individuality and authenticity, celebrating the unique identities of each person, which resonates with Cancer's appreciation for personal connections and emotional expression.
