Pisces

Renowned for its provocative, innovative, and theatrical designs that push the boundaries of fashion, Alexander McQueen is known for its darkly romantic aesthetic and impeccable craftsmanship.

Pisces are known for their creativity, sensitivity, and affinity for the mystical and ethereal, which aligns well with Alexander McQueen's designs. McQueen's brand often incorporates elements of fantasy, romanticism, and dark elegance, reflecting the Piscean inclination towards imagination and emotional depth.
